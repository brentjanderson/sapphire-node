Sapphire

Boldly going where no browser has gone before!

What is Sapphire?

Sapphire is a meager attempt to recreate the magic of the Space Center in Pleasant Grove on the web.

While many aspects of a live mission cannot be recreated in-browser only, the hope is that stories truly are the key to an educational and engaging experience.

What's Sapphire made of?

In an attempt to be really cool, Sapphire is built on NodeJS, ExpressJS, Socket.IO, EJS, and a score of other libraries. Special thanks goes to everyone that has contributed to these projects.

An extra special thank you is in order to Vinay Agarwal for putting together node-backbone on GitHub.
